This is about my and my mum's trip to Paris in France.Â 


    First Day

    • Left home to go to airport
      • Boarded the flight to reach the Paris airport
    • Collected our luggage an went to the hotel via taxi
    • Reached the hotel, collected the key and headed to our room
    • Found out the metro wasn't wheelchair accessible
    • Ate takeaway for dinner and went to bed

    Second Day

    • Woke up and got the wheelchair
    • Got an Uber to the meeting place to the Eiffel Tower
    • Walked and reached the 2nd Level of the Eiffel Tower
    • Left to go for a long walk to see many shops and sites
    • Took many pictures of the city and bought some macaroons
    • Headed to the River Seine Cruise port and went on a Cruise
    • Got an uber back to the hotel
    • Went to get takeout and to bed

    Third Day

    • Woke up early to eat breakfast
    • Tried to get a Uber but took the bus
    • Reached the ground level of the Louvre
    • Saw many exhibits and took pictures
    • Passed the queue to see the Mona Lisa
    • Left the Louvre museum
    • Ended up walking around the city
    • Took the night tour bus
    • Got back to the hotel
    • Ate some snacks and fell asleep

    Fourth Day

    • Woke up at a later time
    • Had a few snacks
    • Headed to the Montparnasse Tower
    • Then, walked to the Luxembourg Gardens
    • Had some bread, cheese and wine
    • Headed to the bus stop, back to the hotel
    • Went to go get dinner

    Fifth Day

    • Left the hotel
    • Went to a cafe to eat breakfast
    • Got onto the bus to go to the airport
    • Went to another cafe
    • Reached the airport
    • Got ready for the flight to go back home
