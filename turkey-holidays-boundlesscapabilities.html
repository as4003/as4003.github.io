    Attraction Sites in Turkey

    • GetYourGuide Link

      • These are a few of the Attraction Sights that can be visited:


    • Hot Air Balloon Trip: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • A hot air balloon consists a bag, which contains heated air and a basket to ride in.
      • There are many spectacular views that can be seen from the hot air balloon.
      • The views can include different landscapes that have been sculpted by erosion over many years.
      • People can see many villages and items from the hot air balloon.
      • Most hot air balloons aren't very accessible, since people would have to climb into basket.
      • The hot air balloons can be accessible with someone else helping to carry the person into the basket.
      • This is very interesting and scenic.


    • Boat Cruise / Gulet Yacht: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • This cruise is quite accessible and luxurious.
      • Once entering there is an entrance for the tickets.
      • The guide puts a ramp down for wheelchair users to access the boat.
      • There is seating on the top level which has many scenic views.
      • Everything can be seen from the seating, with the attractions between Europe to Asia.
      • It is easy to navigate around on the boat to view all the architectures and artifacts.
      • The sceneries are excellent and very interesting.
