    Attraction Sites in Tenerife

    • GetYourGuide Link

      • These are a few of the Attraction Sights that can be visited:


    • Dolphin/Whale Watching: Link (Needs Advance Booking for Guided Tours)

      • This is exceptionally accessible.
      • On the southern coast of Tenerife.
      • The boats can be made adaptable to be wheelchair accessible.
      • These trips can be accessible and very scientifically informative to learn about whales and dolphins.


    • Siam Park: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • The Siam Park is a well-known water park.
      • There are accessible facilities for disabled use.
      • There are quite a few areas which are wheelchair accessible, like the lazy river and wave pool.
      • There is also access to a wheelchair hoist for help into the water.
      • People with mobility issues would do more of the spectating instead of participating.
      • This is an enjoyable and activity-filled place to be.


    • Tiede National Park: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • The Tiede National Park is the largest National park of the Spanish Canary Islands.
      • Tiede National Park has many different adapted paths, which are wheelchair accessible
      • The activities in the National Park can be adventurous and accessible.


    • Jungle Park: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • The Jungle Park is known as Las √Åguilas-Jungle Park.
      • It is a zoological and botanical park on the Canary Island of Tenerife.
      • The park contains several walking paths with tunnels, suspension bridges, waterfalls, lagoons, and caves.
      • Many of the walkways are wheelchair accessible and smooth.
      • The Jungle Park is very informative and scientifically interesting about the animals.
