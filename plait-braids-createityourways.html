PLAIT - BRAIDS:
CREATE IT YOUR WAYS

This is about how to make a plait at home out of a group of strings.

1. Cut 6 (or however many you want) long, different, coloured strings of the same size.

2. Tie the strings together at the top of your pattern.

3. Select 3 groups of 2 strings.

4. Group each of the 2 strings and twine 1 around the other to make a single string.

5. Line each group of strings up on different sides (left, right and centre).

6. Label the left group of strings with a number: 1

7. Label the centre group of strings with a number: 2

8. Label the right group of strings with a number: 3

9. Please pick up the left group of strings and put it over the centre group.

10. Pick up the right group of strings and put it over the centre group of strings.

11. Do this while holding onto the strings already inputted into the pattern.

12. Repeat 9, 10, and 11 until making a plaited string pattern.
