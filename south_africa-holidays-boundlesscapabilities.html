    Attraction Sites in South Africa

    • GetYourGuide Link

      • These are a few of the Attraction Sights that can be visited:


    • Whale Watching: Link (Needs Advance Booking for Guided Tours)

      • This is exceptionally accessible.
      • Whales and dolphins can be seen on the coast of South Africa.
      • The boats can be made adaptable to be wheelchair accessible.
      • These trips can be accessible and scientifically informative to learn about whales and dolphins.


    • Safari & Game-Drives: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • This can be accessible.
      • The Safari is usually an activity/adventure to sightsee various animals.
      • Animals including zebras, giraffes, leopards, cheetahs and lions.
      • The Safari normally includes sitting in a truck and going for a bumpy drive.
      • To get into the truck, people must climb a stair to enter and sit.
      • There is a seatbelt on each seat, and there are no windows to be able to see the outside of the truck.
      • There is a driver to drive the truck and enjoy viewing animals.
      • In the safari camps, there is normally food served for mealtimes.
      • For accessibility reasonings, you would need to tell the safari camp about your mobility.
      • The safari camp can arrange for a wheelchair-accessible vehicle.
      • With securement straps to remain in your wheelchair.
      • This is accessible and has beautiful scenery.


    • Ziplining: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • This is about going on a long ride to access remote areas from a high height, such as a rainforest canopy.
      • Ziplining is a way to transport to gain the chance to observe nature at a fast speed.
      • People doing this will get a harness to attach to the zip line to keep safe.
      • Most ziplines aren't very accessible, but you can ask the guide, and they should be able to help and ensure you can go.
      • The ziplines can be accessible with someone helping lift them.
      • This is very exciting, interesting and scenic.


    • Quad-Biking: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • A quad-bike is a type of all-terrain vehicle that moves at a fast speed on a beaten track road.
      • People can tour, using Quad-bikes, to see more sights in places that are hard to reach.
      • A professional rider can accompany you to ensure you are okay.
      • The Quad-biking can be accessible using other people and mechanisms to help get into the driver's seat and start the journey.
      • The scenery is excellent and interesting, with the key to adventure.
