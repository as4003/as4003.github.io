    Attraction Sites in Kenya

    • GetYourGuide Link

      • These are a few of the Attraction Sights that can be visited:


    • Safari e.g. Maasai Mara/Game-Drives: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • This can be accessible.
      • The Safari is usually an activity/adventure to sightsee a range of different animals.
      • Animals including zebras, giraffes, leopards, cheetahs and lions.
      • The Safari normally includes sitting in a truck and going for a bumpy drive.
      • To get into the truck, people must climb a stair to enter and sit.
      • There is a seatbelt on each seat, and there are no windows to be able to see the outside of the truck.
      • There is a driver to drive the truck and enjoy viewing animals.
      • In the safari camps, there is food served for mealtimes.
      • For accessibility reasonings, you would need to tell the safari camp about your mobility.
      • The safari camp can arrange for a wheelchair-accessible vehicle.
      • With securement straps to remain in your wheelchair.
      • This is accessible and has beautiful scenery.


    • Giraffe Center: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • This is where people can feed and meet the giraffes in the centre.
      • You Can learn about the stories of giraffes at the centre.
      • The person would be given a selection of shell bits to feed the giraffe.
      • Better to get there early as the giraffes are more hungry.
      • You should call the giraffe centre before booking
      • Tell them about your mobility and make sure the centre can adjust to make it more accessible.


    • Mountain Climbing: (Needs Advance Booking)

      • This is about getting ready to climb a mountain.
      • The notes for this can be seen in my Tips & Advice of Climbing.
      • There are many mountains in Kenya
      • Exercise is a big requirement to climb a mountain.
      • This can be made accessible by telling the guide about your ability.
      • The guides should be able to ensure it is accessible.


    • Beaches: (Needs Advance Booking to go to a Hotel)

      • There are many beaches in Kenya, surrounded by the Indian Ocean.
      • Some of the notes for this can be seen in my Tips & Advice for Going On Holiday.
      • The beaches are sometimes inaccessible.
      • This is because of the rocky hills leading towards the beach.
      • The beach can either be a pebbled or a sandy beach, both inaccessible with wheels.
      • The wheels can get stuck in the sand or each of the pebbles.
      • A person can help to get to the sea/ocean.
