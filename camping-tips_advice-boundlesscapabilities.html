These are some helpful pointers to go camping. This is about what to do and not to do and what to bring and not to bring.


Do's

  • Ask someone to come and help, if possible
  • Bring a Tent, even if they have one, it's best to bring one of your own
  • Bring a Sleeping Bag to make sure that you are comfortable while sleeping
  • Take a Sleeping Mat; the ground is sometimes very harsh on your back
  • Ask for help whenever necessary when trying to pitch your tent/unload your stuff
  • Pack food (energy snacks), plastic utensils and dishes
  • Pack enough clothes and toiletries in a large bag to fit everything
  • Do enough exercise to get ready for camping and sleeping in an uncomfortable situation
  • Take extra water to rehydrate
  • Take a first aid kit, including insect repellent, with a whistle
  • Believe that you can do thisÂ 
  • Take a torch/flashlight or a light source
  • Listen to something while camping to keep your mind busy
  • Have a good sleep in the tent

Don't

  • Don't worry about what anyone else thinks or says
  • Don't be scared to ask for assistance/help
  • Try not to think about anything else, not to get worried, and to get enough sleep
