WRITING:
CREATE IT YOUR WAYS

You can do this if you want to write a story or a song/poem at home.

You might need:

  • Pencils
  • Pens
  • Notebooks
  • Writing Paper
  • Thesaurus
  • Dictionary
  • Color Pencils
  • Color Pens
  • Alphabet Stensils (maybe)

You can:



  • Write a story:

    • Think of a topic or subject that inspires you to write about.
    • Start with a sentence and continue.
    • If needed, check the thesaurus for new words to use.
    • After you have finished, check the grammar.
    • Proofread and check each of the sentences.


  • Write a song:

    • Think of something or someone that inspires you.
    • If trying to find something to write about, you can research the word.
    • Find some words relating to your specific inspiration.
    • Think or research for some rhyming words to be used in the song.
    • If needed, check the thesaurus for other words to use.
    • Think of the type of music you want to add to the song.
    • Try to find some lyrics that will go along with the music.


  • Write a poem:

    • Think of something or someone that inspires you.
    • If trying to find something to write about, you can research the word.
    • Find some words relating to your specific inspiration.
    • If needed, check the thesaurus for other words to use.
    • Proofread and check the poem.
