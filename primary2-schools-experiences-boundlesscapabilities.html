    My Experience

    • Started in Year 2
    • Left after Year 6
    • I Did not use my Kaye Walker much but did have it
    • Couldn't move around easily
    • Didn't make many friends easily
    • But, everyone tried to help and talk to me

    Year 2

    • Didn't have a person to care for me
    • But did have help along the way
    • Sat on the bench, in breaks
    • Read the books placed near the bench
    • Easy to get back to class
    • Didn't go in the playground a lot
    • Lessons were straightforward
    • Did not have many friends (but a few) in school

    Year 3 & 4

    • In middle school
    • Had a Learning Support Assistant (LSA)
    • Helped me with many things
    • Classrooms were up the stairs
    • Luckily, the school had a lift
    • Used my Walker
    • Moved around the playground
    • Made friends easily
    • In breaks, I didn't need the LSA
    • Was around a lot of people to help, if needed
    • Took an afterschool activity
    • Didn't have my LSA there
    • Mostly stayed in the library

    Year 5 & 6

    • Started another stage of middle school
    • Had a different Learning Support Assistant (LSA)
    • Helped me a lot with many things
    • Used my Walker for the playground
    • In Year 5, I was in the classroom, on the ground floor
    • Didn't use the lift
    • Form tutor and was an English teacher
    • Confidence and kindness grew
    • Year 6 was in the French class
    • Where I didn't use my Walker
    • There were steps to get into the building
    • The form tutor was a French teacher
    • She allowed me to stay in the class during breaks
    • In breaks, I colour drawings

    Reason Why I Left?

    • Left this school after Year 6
    • To go to secondary school on time
    • Needed to make many friends
    • The school was great since I had a lot of support
