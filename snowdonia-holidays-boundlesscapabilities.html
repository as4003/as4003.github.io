    Attraction Sites in Wales

    • GetYourGuide Link


    • Snowdonia National Park: Link

      • This can be accessible.
      • The national park is free.
      • Taking part in adventure courses, requires advance booking and payment.
      • On maps, it is called Eryri National Park, located on the Snowdonia Mountain.
      • This National Park has brilliant landscapes, which attract many people.
      • This is home to Wales' highest peak and Wales' largest natural lake.
      • People can do many activities in the National Park.
      • They can take walks and do many adventurous activities.
      • The sceneries and sights are beautiful.
      • The walks around the National Park can be very accessible.
      • The National Park has many castles and mountain ranges to visit and sight-see.
      • This is accessible and has beautiful sceneries.


    • Caernarfon Castle: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • This is where people can go to feed and meet the giraffes in the center.
      • Can learn about the stories of giraffes at the center.
      • The person would be given a selection of shell bits to feed the girrafe.
      • Better to get there early as the giraffes are more hungry.
      • You should call the giraffe center before booking
      • Tell them about your mobility and make sure the center can make adjustment to make it more accessible.


    • Mountain Climbing: (Needs Advance Booking)

      • This is about getting ready to climb a mountain.
      • The notes for this can be seen in my Tips & Advice of Climbing.
      • There are many mountains in Kenya
      • Exercise is a big requirement to be able to climb a mountain.
      • This can be made accessible by telling the guide about your ability.
      • The guides should be able to ensure, it is accessible.


    • Beaches: (Needs Advance Booking to go to a Hotel)

      • There are many beaches in Kenya, surrounded by the Indian Ocean.
      • Some of the notes for this can be seen in my Tips & Advice for Going On Holiday.
      • The beaches are sometimes inaccessible.
      • This is because of the rocky hills, leading towards the beach.
      • The beach can either be a pebbled beach or a sandy beach, which are both inaccessible with using wheels.
      • The wheels can get stuck in the sand or each of the pebbles.
      • A person can help to get to the sea/ocean.
