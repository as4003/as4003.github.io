This is about what we did while my family went to Mount Kenya


    First Day

    • Flew to Nairobi
    • Stayed at my parent's friend's house

    Days After

    • Some friends and my family went to climb Mount Kenya
    • My family left early in the morning

    Sweet Waters Campsite

    • Left for the Sweet Waters Serene Camp with six people
    • Stayed in two huts/rooms in the camp
    • Went for Day game drive, had some tea and cake
    • Ate breakfast, lunch and dinner, in the campsite
    • Spent three days in the campsite while going on game drives

    After Campsite

    • Stayed three nights, till everyone came back
    • Packed to go to Malindi on the next day
    • Left to go to the airport

    Malindi

    • Did many different activities
    • Went to the sky diving centre, where some of us went sky diving
    • Spent some time on the beach and in the swimming pool
