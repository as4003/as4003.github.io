    First Day

    • Left home to the train station, with the use of a ramp to go onto the train
    • Got to the airport to go to the shops and board the flight
    • Landed in Rome, where we went into a minibus to enter the baggage claim
    • Arrived at the train station to reach the hotel
    • Went through the shopping centre
    • Entered our room in the hotel, where we unpacked our bags
    • Got ready for dinner at a restaurant near our hotel
    • Chose to go to the Fontana de Trevi (Trevi Fountain)
    • Left the Trevi Fountain to go back to the hotel to fall asleep

    Second Day

    • Got dressed and went for breakfast
    • After we walked to the Colosseum
    • The Colosseum had a lift to view the arena from different floors
    • Left the Coloseum, we went to the Palatine Hill and the Roman Forum
    • Travelled back to the hotel, where we stopped at a restaurant for lunch
    • Once getting back, we had a very relaxing break in the hotel room
    • Went out for dinner and got a glimpse of the Pantheon temple

    Third Day

    • Went to the Pantheon temple, we found out we needed tickets
    • In the temple, I had a chance to wheel myself around
    • We then decided to eat lunch and get some gelato
    • Headed to the Spanish Steps and sat near a fountain
    • Later, we went back to the hotel to relax before eating dinner

    Fourth Day

    • Decided to go to the Vatican Museum and Sistine Chapel
    • Got an audio tour guide for the Sistine Chapel
    • We met a tour guide for the Vatican City

    Fifth Day

    • Reached the airport in Rome and got a chance to get some food
    • Boarded the flight and got back home
