  • - ABOUT ME

This is about how and where I learnt Gujarati.


    About

    • A Hindu language, used in India
    • My mother tongue language
    • Originates from Gujarat, a state in India

    Gujarati Teaching Association

    • Did this with a group, to be with people with similar family histories
    • Began when I was seven years old, with my brothers and my cousins
    • Had two teachers, one for the younger kids and one for the older kids (and two substitutes)
    • Each lesson was at 10 am, every Saturday
    • I joined the younger kids and then moved up
    • Did this at a College
    • I knew the tutors from family functions
    • After a few years, both my brothers left the school
    • I continued to go in with my cousin
    • The school moved to another nearby school
    • Learnt how to read, write and speak in Gujarati

    After

    • Left this school before entering year 10 (when I was 14)
    • Used my walker to get in the classroom
    • Was able to build my communication skills with my grandparents
