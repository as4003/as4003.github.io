This is about how, I did my Bronze DOFE.


    How I Started

    • Truth is: I started because of my brothers
    • Also, because my secondary school was advertising it
    • My friends were doing DOFE too
    • Did it to grow my confidence and independence
    • To gain more friendships

    About

    • Did this with my secondary school

    Volunteering

    • Spent 3 Months
    • Worked as a Library Assistant to help my community
    • This was to gain my independence
    • Used the Kaye Walker to do this

    Physical

    • Spent 3 Months
    • Played Boccia with a group of other people
    • This was to gain my confidence
    • Used the Kaye Walker, while sitting, for this

    Skills

    • Spent 6 Months
    • Learnt a language at home, with a tutor
    • This was to improve my language skills in Spanish
    • Took many notes and learned how to read books in Spanish

    Expedition

    • Asked school, but they wouldn't take me
    • Spent a lot of time planning
    • I Tried to go with someone else, but DOFE said I needed to go with someone who is a DOFE Leader
    • Used a tricycle for my first one
    • Had to do a big search for disability-friendly places that do DOFE Expeditions
    • Found a place in Gloucestershire
    • Did my Expedition in 2019, after my GCSEs
    • Walked in the Forest of Dean, in Gloucestershire
    • Walked with my Walking Frame, but used my wheelchair, when I was tired
    • Met a lot of great people and helpers, who became friends
    • Improved my confidence, independence, motivation and teamwork skills
