  • - ABOUT ME

This is about how I did my Bronze DOFE.


    How I Started

    • My inspiration was to follow my brothers who wer doing the program
    • My secondary school helped me get started
    • Did it to gain more confidence and independence
    • To build more friendships

    Volunteering

    • Spent 3 Months
    • Worked as a Library Assistant to help my community
    • Gained confidence
    • Used the Kaye Walker to do this

    Physical

    • Spent 3 Months
    • Played Boccia with a group of other people
    • Increased my physical independence
    • Used the Kaye Walker, while sitting, for this

    Skills

    • Spent 6 Months
    • Learnt a language at home, with a tutor
    • Improved my language skills in Spanish
    • Took many notes and learned how to read books in Spanish

    Expedition

    • Since my school had no provisions for continuing my award, I had to seek a DofE Leader
    • It was a big task to search for a DofE Leader but my mum and I were determined to not give up
    • Used my tricycle for my trial expedition
    • Had to do a big search for disability-friendly places that do DOFE Expeditions
    • Found a place in Gloucestershire
    • Did my Expedition in 2019 in Gloustershire, after my GCSEs
    • Walked with my Walking Frame, but used my wheelchair, when I was tired
    • Met a lot of great people and helpers, who became friends
    • Improved my confidence, independence, motivation and teamwork skills

    What Did It Improve

    • Confidence
    • Independence
    • Motivation
    • Teamwork Skills
    • Organisation
    • Navigation
    • Communication Skills
    • Language Skills
