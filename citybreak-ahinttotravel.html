CITY BREAK:
   A HINT TO TRAVEL

These are some of the tips to go on a city break with the tips on the homepage on AHintToTravel Website.


These are some general helpful pointers to go to a city break on holiday. To allow you to adequately prepare for your trip, I have prepared a list of hints which you may find useful.


Kit List

  • Enough clothes depending on the weather
  • Suncreen
  • Sunglasses
  • Sun-hat/Cap
  • Insect repellent
  • Tote
  • Aloe vera lotion
  • Comfortable and warm shoes e.g. walking shoes
  • Lip-balm/Chapstick
  • Hand sanitizer

Tips

  • Keep the following things at hand
    • Important documents e.g. passport
    • Spare change of clothes
    • Food and water
    • First aid kit
    • Suncreen
    • Sunglasses
    • Sun-hat/Cap
    • Insect repellent
    • Tote
    • Aloe vera lotion
    • Lip-balm/Chapstick
    • Hand sanitizer
    • Charging cables
    • Power Banks
    • Reading books
    • Headphones/earphones
    • Travel sickness medicine or a paper bag
  • Put on sunscreen and insect repellent, everyday
  • Put aloe vera lotion onto insect bites
  • Put lip-balm onto dry lips
