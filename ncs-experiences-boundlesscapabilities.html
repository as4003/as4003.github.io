Preparation For NCS:


    Research:

    • National Citizen Service Link

      • It was historically founded in 2009 and formally announced in 2010 by Prime Minister David Cameron.
      • After the 2015 general election, the programme was continued under the Conservative government.
      • NCS is currently meant for people, from ages between 15 to 17.
      • The 1st Week is the Adventure Week, where people spend at a activity center.
      • The 2nd Week is known as the University Week, where people spent a week at a university to learn about the journey.
      • The 3rd Week is known as the Charity Week, which is for raising money for a local charity.
      • The 4th and Final Week is the Presentation Week, for planning and presenting to parents to view the presentation about the week.
      • The NCS programme is currently used to grow confidence, life skills, teamwork and strengths.
