This is about how I did in my NCS Weeks.


    How I Started

    • NCS stands for National Citizen Service
    • Normally, for 4 weeks, but I did it for 3 weeks
    • Searched online for an NCS Event
    • Helped me to grow my confidence and independence
    • Gained more friendships
    • Wanted to extend my skills and achievements

    About

    • Located in Harlow
    • 1st Week - Adventure Week
    • 2nd Week - University Week
    • 3rd Week - Charity and Presentation Week
    • Left for Harlow College to meet everyone
    • Only one other girl, rest were boys
    • 4 Helpers in each of the weeks
    • Made use of the wheelchair

    1st Week

    • Adventure Week, with tree-top challenges
    • Stayed in a cabin with the other girl for the first 5 days
    • Did many outdoor activities, e.g. rock climbing and ziplining, etc.
    • Also, did team building exercises with different roping activities
    • Ate in the cafeteria and met up in the hut with everyone in the Harlow team

    2nd Week

    • University Week, to learn about university life
    • Spent 5 days in a University room, at ground level
    • Did many exercises to learn about what to do in university
    • Had meals in different places
    • Made many friendships and met a few people I knew before NCS
    • Had many movie nights and team nights
    • Went to the Gym one time, where we played football and basketball

    3rd Week

    • Charity and Presentation Week, to raise money for a nearby hospice
    • Spent 5 days in Harlow College, where I went home after each day
    • Held different events to raise money for the hospice
    • Arranged the event for people to pay to play PS4 Games
    • Gave out donated snacks and raffle tickets to earn money for charity
    • Went to town to give out wristbands and raise money
    • Prepared a presentation to give to tell the parents about our adventure

    What Did It Improve

    • Confidence
    • Independence
    • Motivation
    • Teamwork Skills
    • Organisation
    • Navigation
    • Communication Skills
    • Presenting Skills
