When I was seven, I went to Scotland, Edinburgh, with my family and grandparents.

    • Drove to Edingburgh, with rest breaks
    • Got to the cottage
    • I had a flu
    • Took a lot of antibiotics
    • Ate dinner and chatted
    • Watched a movie
    • Fell asleep

    Second Day

    • Woke up early
    • Got ready for the climb
    • Took a extra pair of clothes
    • Left the cottage
    • Drove to the mountain
    • Stretched for a bit
    • Started climbing, step by step
    • Held onto both my parents' hands
    • Put my feet in correct positions
    • Took breaks to have food and water
    • Reached the top of the mountain
    • Ate some ice cream
    • Headed back to the cottage
    • Went into the hot tub
    • Ate a big meal for dinner
    • Had an early night

    Third Day

    • Me and my grandparents went for a walk
    • We, then had lunch
    • Went into the hottub
    • Got back into the cottage
    • Got dressed and watched tv
    • My parents and brothers arrived
    • We all ate dinner and went to sleep

    Fourth Day
