  • - ABOUT ME

When I was seven, I went to Scotland, Edinburgh, with my family and grandparents.


    First Day

    • Drove to Edingburgh to reach the cottage
    • Had dinner, watched a movie and fell asleep

    Second Day

    • Woke up early to get ready for the climb
    • Took a extra pair of clothes
    • Left the cottage to drive to the mountain, Ben Arn
    • Stretched for a bit to start climbing, step by step
    • Held onto both my parents' hands while trying to put my feet in correct positions
    • Took extra breaks to have food and water
    • Reached the top of the mountain and went to the cottage
    • Went into the hot tub
    • Ate a big meal for dinner and had an early night

    Third Day

    • Family went to climb Ben Nevis, leaving me with my grandparents
    • Went for a walk and had lunch
    • Went into the hottub, got dressed and watched some TV
    • Then, ate dinner and went to sleep

    Fourth Day

    • Left the cottage to go see landmarks
    • Drove back to England, home
