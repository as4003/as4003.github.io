    About

    • Larger than my previous school
    • Ranges from Level 1 to Level 4
    • State school
    • Boys and Girls school
    • For further Education
    • College
    • A large building with a lift, located near Asda
    • School of about thousands of people
    • 2-5 classes in each course
    • Courses in different places, with different tutors
    • Range of various Courses
    • Had to move around diverse classrooms
    • Many people, still doing their GCSEs, while doing their courses

    My Experience

    • Spent two years
    • Started after I passed all my GCSEs
    • Left after I got a diploma to go to university
    • Used the Kaye Walker all the time
    • To improve my confidence and independence
    • All my classes were on the second level, but there was a lift
    • Used the wheelchair for trips
    • Everyone talked to me, and I made a lot of friendships
    • In the first year, I had Key LSA for most of my classes
    • In the second year, I didn't have a Key LSA, but there were people to help me
    • Were a range of ways to support me
    • Needed help with my bag
    • Had made many friends
    • In lessons, I got to handout notes, and my scribe wrote notes
    • For tests, got to do them in another room
    • Had a scribe, a reader and extra time for tests
    • Spent my breaks in the form room

    Reason Why I Left?

    • Left this school after finishing and achieving a diploma in IT
    • Needed to find a place, to continue doing Computer Science and get my degree
    • Left to go to a nearby university
