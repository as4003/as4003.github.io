SEASIDE HOLIDAY:
   A HINT TO TRAVEL

These are some of the tips to go on a seaside holiday with the tips on the homepage on AHintToTravel Website.


These are some helpful pointers to go to the seaside on holiday. To allow you to adequately prepare for your trip, I have prepared a list of hints which you may find useful.


Kit List

  • Enough clothes e.g. shorts, tank-tops, dress, etc.
  • Suncreen
  • Sunglasses
  • Sun-hat/Cap
  • Insect repellent
  • Tote
  • Aloe vera lotion
  • Bathing suit
  • Ultraviolet (UV) / Sun protection tops
  • Flip-flops/sandals
  • Beach shoes/blanket/mat/tent
  • Beach bag

Tips

  • Keep the following things at hand
    • Important documents e.g. passport
    • Spare change of clothes
    • Food and water
    • First aid kit
    • Beach bag
    • Suncreen
    • Sunglasses
    • Sun-hat/Cap
    • Insect repellent
    • Tote
    • After sun lotion
    • Aloe vera lotion
    • Charging cables
    • Power Banks
    • Reading books
    • Headphones/earphones
    • Travel sickness medicine or a paper bag
  • Put on sunscreen and insect repellent, everyday
  • Change into a bathing suit and a UV top when going to the beach, to save time
  • Put aloe vera lotion onto insect bites or after sun lotion onto sun-burns
