Preparation For Sport Activities:


  • Facilitate assisstance from family/friends to and from the sport venues.
  • Research the sport activity, to take part in the right choice of sport.
  • Ensure you are physically ready for the sport, by doing warm-up exercises before and keeping hydrated.
  • Collect the required equipment to do the sport accurately.
  • The supervisor should be able to customise for your specific requirements e.g. the accessibility.
  • You should inform them on the level of ability to enable them to help you to accomplish this achievement.
  • The sport equipment should be easy to use, for many people to do.
