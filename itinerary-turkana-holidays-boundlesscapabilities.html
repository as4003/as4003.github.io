  • - ABOUT ME

This is about what we did in Turkana, Kenya

    First Day

    • Went to a friend's house to meet the team
    • Gathered to take a picture and put all items in the truck
    • Stepped into the truck to be seated
    • Passed Tikka with different regions in Meru, Kenya
    • Reached Timau to eat lunch and chat
    • Travelled to a hotel in Timau to stretch and see the waterfall
    • Advanced to Nanyuiki, where we made a last stop
    • Passed the Elephant Corridor and the 'Cat and Mouse' rock formation
    • Travelled 8 hours to reach the Sabache Campsite, to spend two nights
    • Set up our tents and unloaded the bags
    • Sat on the side to wait there for everyone else to finish
    • Ate dinner on the first night, which was vegetable stew with rice
    • Had a campfire during dinner, where we discussed a range of topics

    Second Day

    • Most people left early to go climb Mt Ololokwe
    • A few people didn't go, including me
    • We went on a medicine walk to look at how nature cures diseases
    • After, we ate leftovers for lunch
    • Stayed for another night at the campsite
    • Ate dinner and played a bit of football

    Third Day

    • Woke up early at 530 to eat breakfast
    • Went on a journey and planned to go onto Rockslides in the Ngurunit
    • While packing, some of us played cricket
    • Finished packing and loading all the items into the truck
    • We left for Ngurunit
    • On the drive, we saw a lot of towns and male ostriches
    • Had a stop at the Marsabit County welcome sign to take photos
    • Then, stopped off at a petrol station to get water to drink
    • At one point, the truck's wheel got stuck in the sand.
    • Travelled for one hour to reach the Lasamu campsite in Ngurunit
    • Made a campfire and talked while eating dinner

    Fourth Day

    • Woke up early and ate breakfast
    • Went for a walk to look at plants and nature
    • Left the Lasamu campsite, Ngurunit, to go to the Malabo resort in Loiyangalani
    • Made a stop to make donations at a primary school to hand out supplies
    • Reached the campsite in the afternoon, set up our tents and unpacked our bags
    • Gathered in the bar upstairs
    • After, we had a campfire and ate dinner
    • Later on, we fell asleep

    Fifth Day

    • Went to another local school to make more donations
    • Visited the Desert Museum and went boating to an island
    • Later, we went for a swim in Lake Turkana to relax and cool off
    • Then, we reached the campsite and went to the bar
    • After, we ate dinner and rested

    Sixth Day

    • When waking up, we helped to pack up
    • Left to return to the Lasamu campsite, Ngurunit

    Seventh Day

    • Left Ngurunit for Marsabit
    • Went to see a water hole where we saw some animals
    • Then, we reached the AIC Campsite, where we met a family of baboons
    • Gathered to eat dinner

    Eigth Day

    • Left for the Samburu National Park
    • On the way, we to look at ostriches and gazelles
    • Reached the campsite, set up camp and reached a shaded bar
    • After looking around, we ate lunch
    • Some of us went to see the animals in the Samburu National Park

    Ninth Day

    • At Samburu, we had breakfast and went to look at the sunrise
    • Later, played a few games, till getting into the truck
    • Reached Nairobi in about seven hours
