    Attraction Sites in Athens

    • GetYourGuide Link

      • These are a few of the Attraction Sights that can be visited:


    • Acropolis: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • The Acropolis is also known as the Sacred Rock.
      • Considered as the symbol of the ancient greek civilisation and culture.
      • It is one of the most important monument of ancient Greece.
      • The Acropolis Hill and Museum are the main attractions in Athens, Greece.
      • The Acropolis is the religious and administrative center of the city.
      • There is a lift to go up the Acropolis Hill, instead of using the stairs.
      • The Acropolis have excellent views and is wheelchair accessible with the Acropolis being historically interesting.


    • Parthenon Temple: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • The Parthenon is a former temple on the Athenian Acropolis, Greece.
      • The temple was dedicated to Athena, the virgin goddess of wisdom and war.
      • Its decorative sculptures are some of the main points of classical Greek art.
      • The name 'Parthenon' means the house of the virgin.
      • Overall, it is fully accessible with the flat pathways and the Acropolis elevator for wheelchair users.
      • The Parthenon Temple is very interesting and historical.


    • Theatre of Dionysus: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • The Ancient Theatre of Dionysus is regarded as the birthplace of drama.
      • The theatre was dedicated to Dionysus, the god of wine-making and ecstasy.
      • Built for theatrical performances of plays written by the great tragic poets.
      • The plays took place as celebrations to honor Dionysus.
      • The theatre isn't very wheelchair accessible, since there is a cobble-stoned pathway.
      • The theatre has amazing views and is very historically interesting.


    • Monastiraki Flea Market: Link

      • This place has a lot of quirky shopping stops in Athens.
      • It has many different treasures from many places.
      • This area is quite accessible, with the ramps and pathways.
