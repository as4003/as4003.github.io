  • - ABOUT ME

This is about my trip to Wales to climb Snowdonia with my dad, brother, cousins, aunt and uncle, and grandparents.


    First Day

    • Left home
    • Headed to Milton Keynes
    • Picked up my aunt, uncle and cousin
    • Loaded the cars
    • Headed to a hotel/motel
    • Parked the cars on the streets
    • Entered the hotel
    • Kept the walker outside
    • Unpacked and rested in our rooms
    • Went to the gym
    • Headed out of the hotel to go for a walk
    • Got back to the hotel
    • Drove to a place for dinner
    • Went back to the hotel
    • To go to bed and sleep

    Second Day

    • Woke up early
    • Got ready for the climb
    • Took food and water
    • Ate breakfast from the hotel's cafeteria
    • Talked and relaxed for a bit
    • Loaded the cars
    • Left the hotel
    • Reached the mountain
    • Got everything ready
    • Started climbing, step by step
    • Held onto my dad's hand and the walking stick
    • Some of the time, I fell over
    • Had a chance to take rest breaks
    • Reached the halfway point of the mountain
    • Put on some of my music to make my way down
    • Reached the bottom
    • Went back to the car to eat some food
    • Travelled to Birmingham for Macdonalds
    • Went back to Milton Keynes
    • Left to go back home and relax
