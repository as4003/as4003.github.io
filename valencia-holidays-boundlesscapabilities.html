    Attraction Sites in Valencia

    • GetYourGuide Link

      • These are a few of the Attraction Sights that can be visited:


    • Valencia Botanica Gardens: Link

      • This is very accessible.
      • It is free to go to the Botanical Gardens.
      • Can take a walk in the Valenciaâ€™s botanical garden to see the beautiful flora and fauna.
      • It is a destination for when people are interested in nature and plants.
      • This Botanical Garden has various plants and trees, mainly from the Meditteranean flora.
      • A wide range of plants, from aromatic herbs to exotic trees.
      • There are also many themed gardens and museums with pictures of different plants.
      • The pathway can be a bit rough, but there are no steps in the whole garden.
      • This is accessible and has beautiful sceneries.


    • Oceanographic: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • The largest aquarium in Europe.
      • Contains 500 different species of living creatures.
      • This aquarium is scientifically interesting with the flora and fauna.
      • There are some historical facts about the creatures.
      • There are many parts of this aquarium, with different sources of informations.
      • This is fully accessible with the ramps and lift in the oceanarium.


    • Valencia Cathedral: Link (Needs Advance Booking)

      • The Holy Chalice is kept in the Valencia Cathedral.
      • Holds one of the most important pieces of art from the first Spanish Renaissance.
      • It is a Gothic-Style building, which was built on an ancient Roman temple.
      • The cathedral shows both its art and history.
      • This cathedral is also known for protecting valuable treasures like the Holy Chalice.
      • There is a talking tour with the use of headphones.
      • This is very accesible with there being ramps, instead of steps.


    • Plaza de Toros de Valencia: Link

      • The Monument Plaza Toros of Valencia was built between 1850 and 1860.
      • Built on the area of an ancient Bullring of the city.
      • Plaza Toros of Valencia is 1st class Arena, for hosting popular events.
      • This place is very accessible, with a suitable pathway.
