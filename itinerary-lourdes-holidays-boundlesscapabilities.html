This is about what we did on my trip to Lourdes in France 


    Before Trip

    • Chosen to go to Lourdes, with group
    • There was a lot of preparation and meetings
    • Went as a part of the HCPT Pilgrimage
    • Had many small meetings with the group when discussing the programme

    First Day

    • Left early in the morning to get to the coach
    • Got into the coach to sit, to go to the airport
    • The coach parked outside the area of the airport
    • Went to check-in at the airport
    • Left to board the flight, where I slept throughout the flight
      • ​
    • Reached the airport in France, where we went to the hotel
    • Gathered in the lounging area of the hotel
    • Left the hotel to walk around
    • Leaders told us to go to bed and rest till tomorrow

    Second Day

    • Ate breakfast and gathered in the lounge area of the hotel
    • They told us about Saint Bernadette with visions of the Virgin Mary

    Other Days

    • On one of the days, we visited the Grotto
    • On another day, we went to visit the Baths
    • On one other evening, we went to the Torchlight Procession
    • We also went to the old house of Saint Bernadette
    • On Thursday morning, the Trust Mass began for celebrations
    • Gathered to pray and read the individual prayers with hymns
    • On one night, we did a talent show
    • Went to see the icy mountain and surroundings of the area
    • Got a chance to go for a ride on a horse or donkey, near a cafe
