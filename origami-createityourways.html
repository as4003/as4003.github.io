ORIGAMI:
CREATE IT YOUR WAYS

These are ways to make some origami items at home.

To make origami items, you will need:

  • Paper cut into a square
  • Tape (maybe)
  • Scissors (maybe)
  • Pen (if needed)

You can:


  • Find different items to make.
  • Decide which item you want to make.
  • Do some research to make the item:
    • Watch a video.
    • Read instructions online.
    • Get a book with instructions.
  • Set all your required items to be accessible.
  • Add tape if necessary to keep the required folds in place.
  • Place the made items in a box to keep them in place.
