These are some helpful pointers to avoid falling. To allow for you to easily avoid falling, I have prepared do's and dont's which you may find useful


Do's

  • Always feel free to ask for help, where available. Everyone needs help at some point, it's not a strange thing
  • If applicable, don't forget to have any support equipment with you
  • When possible, make sure you take time to rest, to prevent a mishap
  • Keep the following things on hand:
    • A first aid Kit
    • A travel ice pack
  • Loosen any stiffness before you walk
  • Do a first aid course
  • Install handlebars wherever required (especially in the bathroom)
  • Try to create a positive atmosphere, if you fall
  • Try and ask for someone to move things out of your way to avoid tripping
  • Put carpet, wherever possible
  • Do a risk assessment on the place, you're going to
  • Take risks, if you think you'll be fine

Don't

  • Worry about what anyone else thinks or says
  • Be scared to ask for assistance/help
  • Walk when there is something in your way
  • Get distracted while you walk
  • Reach out for things to hold onto
  • Rest on moving things, e.g. doors
