These are some helpful pointers to avoid falling. This is about what to do and not to do.


Do's

  • Ask someone to come and help, if you fall
  • Bring a walking stick or different types of equipment for support, if prone to falling
  • Ask for help whenever neccessary
  • Rest if you need, to prevent a mishap
  • Carry a first aid kit and a travel ice pack
  • Stretch before walking to make sure you are fit to walk
  • Do a first aid course
  • Install handlebars everywhere required (especially in the bathroom)
  • Put handlebars near tiles (if you fall on floor tiles, it could hurt more)
  • Install handrails on both sides of the staircase
  • Install bannister bars to enter your room/house
  • Try to create a positive atmosphere, if you fall
  • Try and ask for someone to move things out of your way to avoid tripping
  • Put carpet, wherever possible
  • Do a risk assessment on the place, you're going to
  • Take risks, if you think you'll be fine

Don't

  • Don't worry about what anyone else thinks or says
  • Don't be scared to ask for assistance/help
  • Do not walk when there is something in your way
  • Try not to get distracted while you walk
  • Try not to reach out for things to hold onto
  • Try not to rest on moving things, e.g. doors
