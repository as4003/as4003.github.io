  • - ABOUT ME

This is about how my parents and I did the Thames Bridges Trek with my wheelchair.


    What We Did

    • For a charity via the ultra challenge website
    • A 25Km walk, covering 16 bridges
    • Me and my parents got the train
    • Went to the starting point of the Trek
    • Got t-shirts with the name and a map
    • Took photos of us
    • Talked about the walk
    • Travelled along the bridges
    • Got pushed and wheeled myself in the wheelchair
    • Many stairs along the way
    • Got out of the wheelchair to walk the stairs

    Bridges

    • The Putney Bridge
    • The Fulham Railway Bridge
    • The Wandsworth Bridge
    • The Battersea Bridge
    • The Albert Bridge
    • The Chelsea Bridge
    • The Vauxhall Bridge
    • The Lambeth Bridge
    • The Westminster Bridge
    • The Golden Jubilee (Hungerford) Bridge
    • The Waterloo Bridge
    • The Blackfriars Bridge
    • The Millenium Bridge
    • The Southwark Bridge
    • The London Bridge
    • The Tower Bridge

    On The Walk

    • Took many photos
    • Had many rest breaks
    • Had some ice cream and snacks
    • Went to the park

    Finished The Trek

    • In last place
    • Was happy to have built up our strength
    • Were given our medals with prosseco
    • Told we could rest and had a cupcake
    • Left to go back to the train station
    • Went home
