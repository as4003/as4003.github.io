These are some helpful pointers to do/go climbing. This is about what to do and not to do and what to bring and not bring.


Do's

  • Ask someone to come and help, if possible
  • Bring a walking stick or different types of equipment for support
  • Ask for help whenever neccessary
  • Try to have a companion
  • Rest whenever possible
  • Take an extra pair of clothes
  • Take a first aid kit with the essentials
  • Do enough exercise to get ready for climbing
  • Take a lot of food and water to regain energy
  • Believe that you can do thisÂ 
  • Listen to something while you are climbing to keep your mind busy
  • Stretch in the rest breaks to relax
  • Take regular breaks for short periods
  • Have a good sleep the night before to get ready for the climb
  • Go at your own pace of moving

Don't

  • Don't worry about what anyone else thinks or says
  • Don't be scared to ask for assistance/help
  • Try not to rest anywhere too dangerous
  • Don't to keep up with anyone, to avoid falling
