These are some helpful pointers to do/go climbing. To allow you to adequately prepare for your climbing trip/activity, I have prepared a few lists of do's and dont's which you may find useful.


Kit List

  • Food
  • Extra blankets
  • Plastic utensils and dishes
  • Extra water to rehydrate
  • Insect repellent
  • Torch/flashlight or light source
  • Extra set of clothes
  • First aid kit
  • Support equipment or walking stick
  • Rehydration Salts

Do's

  • Always feel free to ask for help, where available. Everyone needs help at some point, it's not a strange thing
  • If applicable, don't forget to take any support equipment or walking stick along with you
  • It would be safer to have someone with you during the climb or hike
  • When possible, make sure you take the time to rest
  • Keep the following things at hand
    • Spare change of clothes
    • Rehydration Salts
    • Food and water
    • A first aid kit
  • Loosen any stiffness in the breaks before and during the hike
  • Listen to something while you are climbing to keep your mind busy
  • Take regular breaks for short periods
  • Have a good sleep the night before to get ready for the climb
  • Feel free to move at your own pace to maximise the experience

Don't

  • Worry about what anyone else thinks or says
  • Be scared to ask for assistance/help
  • Pick just any area to rest
  • Go at a fast pace in order to keep up with people
