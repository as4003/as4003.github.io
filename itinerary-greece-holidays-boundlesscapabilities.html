This is about what we did on our trip to Athens and Crete in Greece. 


    First Day

    • Landed in Athens
    • Took taxi to hotel
    • Walked to a restaurant for dinner
    • Some family friends met us in this restaurant
    • Returned to the hotel and fell asleep

Second Day

  • Woke up, ate breakfast and got dressed
  • Got ready go to Acropolis with a borrowed wheelchair
  • Walked to the ΣΤ.ΕΥΑΓΓΕΛΙΣΜΟΥ (the metro station)
  • Reached Acropolis and walked to the Monument Entrance
  • Took a quick visit to the Hill to see the Neoclassical architecture
  • Visited the Dionysus Theater with the New Acropolis Museum
  • Walked to Agora and to the Monastiraki flea market
  • Ate lunch and went back to the hotel
  • Left for dinner and went to the hotel

Third Day

  • Woke up, went to eat breakfast and headed to the metro station
  • Walked to the Panathenaic Stadium and took an audio tour
  • Walked to the Leof Vasileos Alexandrou
  • Went for a picnic at the national gardens, and relaxed
  • Returned back to the hotel to pack for Crete

Fourth Day

  • Left for the hotel
  • Flew to Crete and reached the Crete hotel
  • Met other family friend to eat dinner

Days After

  • Went swimming and played some games
  • Play games before going to sleep
  • Went to the water park, via public bus, on one of the days
  • Went to the football, basketball and volleyball courts
  • On one day, we went to a museum in Crete, Greece
  • Played some cards and poker
  • Next, we headed to the airport
  • We left from Greece Airport to Heathrow Airport
