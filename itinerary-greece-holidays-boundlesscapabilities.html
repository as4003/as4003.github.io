  • - ABOUT ME

This is about what we did on our trip to Athens and Crete in Greece. 


    First Day

    • Me and my family flew from Heathrow Airport to Athens on a British Airways airline
    • We landed in Athens in the afternoon
    • Took a taxi and reached the Hilton hotel in the evening
    • After unpacking, we walked to a restaurant for dinner
    • Ordered some sandwiches with salad
    • Four family friends met us in this restaurant
    • After eating, we went back to the hotel and fell asleep

Second Day

  • Woke up and ate breakfast together
  • After getting dressed, we met on the ground floor to get ready to go to Acropolis
  • We also waited for a wheelchair for me
  • Walked to the ΣΤ.ΕΥΑΓΓΕΛΙΣΜΟΥ (the metro station)
  • Took the M3 to Monastiraki Metro Station, then took a 10-minute walk to the Acropolis
  • Reached Acropolis by using the Acropolis subway station
  • Walked to the Monument Entrance
  • Took a quick visit to the Hill, where we took some photos
  • Saw the Neoclassical architecture
  • Visited two sites, including the Dionysus Theater with the New Acropolis Museum
  • Had some ice cream somewhere local
  • Walked to Agora, walked to the Monastiraki flea market
  • Ate lunch and took the metro from Monastiraki station to the hotel
  • After eating dinner, we walked back to the Hilton hotel

Third Day

  • Woke up, went to breakfast to eat and headed to the metro station
  • Walked to the Panathenaic Stadium from the metro, where we took pictures
  • We had an audio tour of the Stadium to tell us the history
  • Then, walked to the Leof Vasileos Alexandrou
  • Took images of the Acropolis, the Parthenon, Filopappos Hill, Temple of Olympian Zeus and Zappeion
  • After, we went to take a picnic at the national gardens, and relaxed
  • Left to reach the hotel, to rest and pack for Crete, till dinner

Fourth Day

  • Got ready to go to the airport to board the flight
  • Reached the Crete hotel, where we met other people
  • Later, we went to dinner to eat

Days After

  • Had breakfast
  • Went swimming
  • Had Lunch
  • Went swimming and played some games
  • Had Dinner
  • Play games before going to sleep
  • Went to the water park, via public bus, on one of the days
  • On some of the days, we went to football, basketball and volleyball courts
  • On one day, we went to a museum in Crete, Greece
  • On the last day, we played some cards and poker
  • Next, we headed to the airport
  • We left from Greece Airport to Heathrow Airport
