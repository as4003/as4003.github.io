LISTENING TO AUDIOBOOK/PODCAST:
CREATE IT YOUR WAYS

You can do this if you listen to audiobooks/podcasts at home.

You might need:

  • Audiobook/Podcast
  • Headphones
  • Earphones

You can:


  • Find your favourite theme/topic.
  • Find different audiobooks/podcasts related to a specific theme.
  • Download the audiobook/podcast.
  • Listen to the audiobook/podcast in a calm setting.
