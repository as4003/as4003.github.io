This page is about me and my reason for creating this website.

My name is Avika Shah, and I was born in 2003. My family consists of me, my two brothers (one twin and one three years older than me), my mother and father.

I got diagnosed with the disability of Cerebral Palsy Ataxia (in 2013, when I had just turned ten years old), which means I have difficulty with my balance and coordination.

I also have trouble walking and doing small movements with my hands.

I have many reasons for creating this website. I thought it would be good for different people to know that people with disabilities can do many different activities. I hope to inspire people to do many things they think are impossible. Mainly, I want to explain and tell people that people with any disability can do anything that a non-disabled person can do.

I also got inspired to design this website because I know a lot of families want to do different things that they think others in their family (with different certain abilities) cannot do. Still, people with abilities would like the chance/choice to try these activities.
