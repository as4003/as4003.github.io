    How I Started

    • Was near my house
    • Where I could start my second secondary school
    • When I got my EHCP Plan
    • I entered because I had an EHCP (Education, Health, Care Plan)
    • This was a very equipped school
    • In this school, enough support
    • We visited this school with this
    • Met the Head of the Learning Support Assistant (LSA) Department
    • They let me in

    About

    • Much larger than my previous school
    • Ranges from Year 7 to Year 13
    • State school
    • Boys and Girls school
    • Catholic School
    • Secondary school
    • A lot of fields and buildings
    • School of about thousands of people
    • Hundreds of people in each year group
    • About 9-10 classes each year in different places
    • Range of various subjects
    • Had to move around diverse classrooms
    • With long distances to walk

    My Experience

    • Started in Year 9
    • Left after Year 11, GCSEs
    • Used the Kaye Walker all the time
    • To improve my confidence and independence
    • All my classes were at ground level
    • Used the wheelchair for school trips and sometimes P.E.
    • Everyone talked to me, and I made a lot of friendships
    • Had Key LSA for all of my classes
    • Sat near the door, in all my classes
    • Used a range of ways to support me
    • Needed help with my bag
    • Moved from rooms after a few days
    • Had made friends in both form rooms
    • New form foom was near the institution unit
    • Spent a lot of time in the institution unit (room 4)
    • Brought packed lunch to school every day
    • Played Boccia and basketball in P.E. lessons
    • Met most friends in the institution unit, from different age groups
    • In lessons, I got to handout notes, and my scribe wrote notes
    • For tests, got to do them in another room
    • Had a scribe, a reader and extra time for tests

    Year 9

    • Found out I wanted to study I.T./Computer Science
    • Because of a Youth Connections Lady's help
    • Used both my walker and wheelchair

    Year 10 & 11

    • Used my walker
    • So,I was not restricted
    • Had a lot of revision periods and exams in Year 11

    Prom

    • Before GCSE exams
    • First, I didn't want to go because of the scare of choking
    • The head of year asked me why I wasn't going
    • She then communicated with my mum to find a way for me to go
    • Went shopping and found a dress and shoes
    • On Prom, I entered the big hall
    • Went to sit down
    • Couldn't get up and dance
    • Was at the right corner table
    • Couldn't walk to the dance floor
    • Left at 11 pm, (I was scared to ask my mum to come early)

    Reason Why I Left?

    • Left this school after Year 11, GCSEs
    • During Years 10 & 11, I found out where I could go to get a full education
    • Needed to find a place to continue I.T. and get my diploma
    • To go to a nearby college
    • The school didn't have a program of A-LEVELs to do Computer Science
