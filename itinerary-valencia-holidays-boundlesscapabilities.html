  • - ABOUT ME

This is about what we did on my trip to Valencia in Spain


    First Day

    • Left home with my parents dropping us off at the train station
    • Arrived at the airport and checked in
    • Boarded the flight and grabbed some snacks and food
    • Reached the Valencia airport and got a taxi to the AirBnB
    • Saw the rooms and different areas in the AirBnB
    • Left the Airbnb (apartment)
    • Went to get some lunch from the market
    • Then, went to the Botanica Gardens to take pictures
    • Left the Botanica Gardens, to go to a restaurent
    • Finished dinner and headed back to the apartment
    • Went to go to sleep

    Second Day

    • Woke up and left our rooms to eat breakfast
    • Left to go to the museum and gallery, looked at historic artefacts
    • Left to go for lunch at the market
    • Went to the art museum to see all the paintings
    • Then, we went to go to dinner to eat paella
    • After, we left to go to the apartment

    Third Day

  • Left the apartment to go to the marine museum
  • After, we went through the market to find food and drinks
  • Ate dinner and went to the apartment
  • Later, we played some drinking games

Fourth Day

  • Had a big breakfast
  • Took a walk around Valencia
  • Reached the shopping centre
  • Then, headed back to the apartment
  • Played card games and drank drinks
  • Then, went to pack and fall asleep

Fifth Day

  • Woke up, and packed
  • Left the apartment and went to a cafe
  • Got ready for the flight and headed back home
