WITH A DISABILITY:
   A HINT TO TRAVEL

These are some of the tips to go on a holiday with a disability and the tips on the homepage on AHintToTravel Website.


These are some general helpful pointers to go on a holiday with a disability. To allow you to adequately prepare for your trip, I have prepared a list of hints which you may find useful.


Kit List

  • Walking aids
  • Wheelchair or different aids
  • Extra first aid kits
  • Ice packs

Tips

  • Always feel free to ask for help where available. Everyone needs help at some point; it's not a strange thing
  • Don't be scared to ask for assistance/help
  • Keep the following things at hand
    • Essential documents, e.g. passport
    • Spare change of clothes
    • Food and water
    • A first aid kit
    • Charging cables
    • Power Banks
    • Reading books
    • Headphones/earphones
    • Travel sickness medicine or a paper bag
    • An extra scarf to go to temples
  • Be vigilant at night to avoid danger
  • Don't pick just any area to rest
  • Stretch in the rest breaks to gain strength
  • Don't stay in the same position for too long to avoid getting numb
  • Listen to something to keep your mind busy
  • Go to the toilet before making the journey
  • Don't worry about what anyone else thinks or says


For further information please visit this website: Boundless Capabilities.
