YOGA:
CREATE IT YOUR WAYS

You can do this if you do yoga at home.

You might need:

  • Yoga Mat
  • Calming Music
  • Cushion/Pillow

You can:


  • Do these stretches:


    • Downward-Facing Dog Pose - Get down on all fours and lift your knees to start to stand on the hands and feet while holding.

    • Cobra Stretch / Reclining Back-Bending Pose - Lie on your stomach and hold yourself up with your arms while arching your back backwards and keeping your knees on the ground.

    • Child Pose - Get down on all fours and sit on your knees while arching your back towards the ground.

    • Cat Cow Pose - Get on to all fours and create a cat (by arching the spine towards the ground) and a cow (by arching the spine upwards).

    • Triangle Pose - While standing, stretch your legs apart and bend your back towards the left/right by touching your knee with your left/right hand.

    • Bridge Pose - Lie on your back, bend your knees towards your bottom, and lift your bottom to make an arch shape.

    • Tadasana / Mountain Pose - Stand upright while inhaling and raising the arms in front of you towards the sky while locking your fingers.

    • Standing Forward Fold - Stand upright while arching your back and touching your toes with your fingers.

    • Lunge - Stand and put one leg in front while bending the front knee towards the front.

    • Knee to Chest - Lie on your back and bend your knees towards your chest while holding your knees.

    • Plank Pose - Lie on your stomach and hold yourself up with your arms while arching your back backwards.

    • Leg Up the Wall - Lie on your back and lift one leg towards the sky.
