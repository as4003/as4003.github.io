These are some helpful pointers to go on holiday. To allow you to adequately prepaare for your trip , I have prepared a few lists of do's and dont's which you may find useful.


Kit List

  • Neck Pillow (if needed)
  • Food
  • Extra blanket
  • Enough luggage
  • Enough clothes
  • Enough toiletries
  • First aid kit
  • Support equipment
  • Important documents e.g. passports
  • Travel sickness medicine or a paper bag

Do's

  • Check advice on travel websites and look up information in books
  • Get travel and health insurance
  • Always feel free to ask for help, where available. Everyone needs help at some point, it's not a strange thing
  • Give these items to your campanion:
    • Copy of your itinerary
    • Copy of your travel insurance
  • Have a good sleep, the night before to get ready for the journey
  • Keep the following things at hand:
    • Important documents e.g. passport
    • Spare change of clothes
    • Food and water
    • A first aid kit
    • Travel sickness medicine or a paper bag
    • Print out of your itinerary
  • Be vigilant at night, to avoid danger
  • Leave home early to take occasional breaks on/before the journey
  • Take regular breaks for short periods
  • Stretch in the rest breaks to gain strength
  • Believe that you will be okay on the journey
  • Listen to something to keep your mind busy
  • Go to the toilet before making the journey
  • Deposit important documents in a safe e.g. passports

Don't

  • Worry about what anyone else thinks or says
  • Be scared to ask for assistance/help
  • Pick just any area to rest
  • Stay in the same position for too long, to avoid getting numb
