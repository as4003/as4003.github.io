These are some helpful pointers to go on holiday. This is about what to do and not to do, and what to bring and not to bring.


Do's

  • Ask someone to come and help with luggage and travel, if possible
  • Bring enough luggage and toiletries for the holiday
  • Ask for help whenever neccessary
  • Leave home early to take occasional breaks on/before the journey
  • Take an extra pair of clothes on hand
  • Go to the toilet before making the journey
  • Take a lot of food and water on your trip, for energy
  • Believe that you will be okay on the journey
  • Be vigilant at night, to avoid danger
  • Check advice on travel websites and look up information in books
  • Deposit important documents in a safe e.g. passports
  • Carry a first aid kit
  • Take travel sickness medicine and paper bags, if increase of sickness
  • Listen to something while travelling, avoid travel sickness
  • Stretch in the rest breaks to gain strength
  • Have a good sleep, the night before to get ready for the journey

Don't

  • Don't worry about what anyone else thinks or says
  • Don't be scared to ask for assistance/help
  • Don't stay in the same position for to long, to avoid getting numb
  • Try not to rest anywhere too dangerous
