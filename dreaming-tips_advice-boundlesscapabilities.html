These are some helpful pointers to achieve dreams. To allow for you to easily gain different accomplishments, I have prepared do's and dont's which you may find useful


Do's

  • Always feel free to ask for help, where available. Everyone needs help at some point, it's not a strange thing
  • Some Research, before trying
  • If applicable, don't forget to have any support equipment with you
  • When possible, make sure you take time to rest, to prevent a mishap
  • Keep the following things on hand:
    • A first aid Kit
    • A travel ice pack
  • Try to challenge yourself
  • Rest whenever needed
  • Use your bravery and courage
  • Remember: you can do anything, even if it seems impossible
  • Use your energy, pace yourself
  • Believe in yourself
  • Fulfill your dreams (even if it seems ridiculous)
  • Talk to many people for their perspective on these experiences
  • Use unique and great websites, with useful information
  • Do a risk assessment
  • Take a risk
  • Be sensible
  • Remember to show your true emotions.
  • Treat everyone better than they deserve.

Don't

  • Worry about what anyone else thinks or says
  • Be scared to ask for assistance/help
  • Let anyone make you feel insecure or obselete
  • Worry about ruining anything
  • Be afraid to do anything new
  • Let people bother you
  • Worry about falling, have fun
  • Let anybody take advantage of you
