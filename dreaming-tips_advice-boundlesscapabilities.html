These are some helpful pointers to do everything you want to accomplish.


Do's

  • Ask someone to help, if needed
  • Some Research, before trying
  • Try to challenge yourself
  • Rest whenever needed
  • Use your bravery and courage
  • Remember: you can do anything, even if it seems impossible
  • Use your energy, pace yourself
  • Believe in yourself
  • Fulfill your dreams (even if it seems ridiculous)
  • Talk to many people for their perspective on these experiences
  • Use unique and great websites, with useful information
  • Do a risk assessment
  • Take a risk
  • Be sensible
  • Remember to show your true emotions.
  • Treat everyone better than they deserve.

Don't

  • Don't worry about what anyone else thinks or says
  • Don't be scared to ask for assistance/help
  • Don't worry about falling, have fun
  • Don't be afraid to do anything new
  • Don't worry about ruining anything
  • Don't let anyone make you feel insecure or obselete
  • Try not let people bother you
  • Don't let anybody take advantage of you
